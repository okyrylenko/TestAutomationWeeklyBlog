Overall, I have been in Software Quality Assurance field for almost 10 years as of early 2021. I started my career as a manual tester and moved toward automation about 5 years ago. As in any field, especially in technology a person is constantly has to learn and upgrade the existing skills. I like to spend a couple hours a day on learning new technology. My day usually start at 7 am and spend 2 hours on learning. My work starts at 9 am and that is when I completely focus on it.

The reason why I am creating this blog is to help myself in learning process. I would be happy if some of my posts will help others but that is not my main goal, nor to commercialize my blog and make money out of it. I think if a person cannot explain in plain English a topic, a person does know or does not understand a subject to the full extend. In my experience, a lot of times someone thinks he or she knows the solution to a problem, but when a person starts writing on how to solve a problem, there are a lot of small aspects that come up and additional research needs to be done. The additional research helps a person to gain a deeper understanding of a topic. Combining those two reasons, I decided to start my blog; as a result, I can put on a paper in a plain English what I know or learnt and if I do not understand something, I am required to do additional research. The posts will be written in narrative form without a single line of code.
